SmartKnife™ Pro

A knife with Wi-Fi. When connected to BreadGPT, it vibrates to guide your hand to the optimal slice angle in real-time. When not connected — either because you don't have a subscription or because it has dropped Wi-Fi again — it is a knife that cost $799. Our firmware engineer is working on the connection issue. Our firmware engineer has also given notice. We are hiring a new firmware engineer. See Careers.

  • ✓ Haptic angle guidance (when connected)

  • ✓ Dishwasher safe (the handle; not the circuit board)
  • ✓ 6-hour battery with haptics on; 4 days without
  • ⚠ Currently drops connection every 15-20 minutes
  • ⚠ Do not use as a regular knife and expect haptics to work — it is not a regular knife, it is an $799 IoT device that also cuts things

SliceBot™ (Working Title)

An autonomous bread-slicing robot. You put the loaf in. It analyzes, identifies the optimal slice trajectory, and slices. No human input required. The prototype exists. It has sliced 14 loaves. Seven of those slices were acceptable. Three were "interesting." Four have been classified as incidents. We are on incident report 4b. We are not giving up on this.
