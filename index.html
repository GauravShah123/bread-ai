Optimal Slice Angle™
We analyze 847 variables to find the perfect slice angle. The answer is always 87.4°. A normal person would call this "straight." We call it a breakthrough. There is a 14-page whitepaper. Nobody has read it.

Crust Sentiment Analysis
Is your crust happy? Sad? Guarded? Ours detects 7 emotions. The most common is "neutral." The rarest is "joyful." We have detected it once, in a brioche in Lyon. We cried.

Staleness Prediction
Know exactly when your bread goes stale — down to the minute. After $380K in compute costs, our model answers with remarkable consistency. The answer is Thursday. We are looking into it.
