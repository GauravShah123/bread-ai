Hardware & Add-ons

The bread intelligence ecosystem.
Everything costs more than you'd expect.

🔪

SmartKnife™ Pro

$799

A knife with Wi-Fi. USB-C charging. Haptic feedback guides you to the optimal slice angle. Currently drops connection every 15-20 minutes. We are working on this. Without a subscription, it functions as a regular knife for $799. We understand this is a lot for a knife. We are working on that too.

📡

IoT Sensor Kit (3-pack)

$299

Three sensors for full loaf triangulation. Attaches via food-safe adhesive. Reusable. DO NOT BAKE WITH THE SENSORS ATTACHED. We have said this 47 times across our documentation. Seven of our beta testers baked the sensors. To be fair, we only have eight beta testers. We improved the warning font size after incident 4.

👨‍💻

Onboarding: The Chad Call

$2,500

A 4-hour Zoom with Chad. Hour 1: product walkthrough. Hour 2: Q&A. Hour 3: Chad talks about bread in general. Hour 4: Chad shows you Leviticus on camera. Leviticus is a sourdough starter. He is fed twice a day. He is doing extremely well. Customers who have booked this call report it as "a lot" but give it 4.2 stars on average.

Annual Bread Intelligence Report

$499/yr
